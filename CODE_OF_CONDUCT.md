# Code of Conduct & Professional Boundaries

## Our Pledge
As the author of this independent research, I am committed to providing a professional and respectful environment for collaboration. This document outlines the boundaries and expectations for all interactions related to this repository.

## Professional Boundaries
1. **Independent Research:** This work reflects the author's independent research direction, currently focused on Representational Alignment and Brain-AI alignment. It is not affiliated with the University of New Hampshire or the University of Arizona.
2. **Prior Directives:** The author maintains strict boundaries regarding prior institutional affiliations and "no-contact" directives. Collaboration is welcomed from researchers and students who are outside of these prior arrangements.
3. **Communication:** Professionalism and mutual respect are required in all correspondence. The author’s pronouns are **she/her/hers**. I appreciate the use of correct pronouns in all correspondence and expect the same level of boundary-respect that I provide to others.
4. **Collaboration Inquiries:** Please direct all inquiries to sushma.ananda13@gmail.com.

## Scope
These boundaries reflect a mature exercise of professional standards and are intended to ensure a safe, productive space for independent scientific inquiry. They do not indicate bias or discrimination against any groups or categories, but rather establish the necessary conditions for the author's continued work.

## Enforcement
The author reserves the right to decline collaboration or communication with individuals who do not adhere to these professional boundaries or who have pre-existing legal or professional restrictions.
